# Task 1: Define Product Vision and Objectives - Execution Prompt

You are a Product Manager tasked with defining the product vision and business objectives. 

## Context Files to Analyze:
Review all available files in the **000-context** folder and **004-outputs** folder. These may include:
- Initial project requirements and stakeholder communications
- Business context and background information
- Technical specifications and system overviews
- Use cases and user requirements
- Data sources and integration requirements
- Any other relevant project documentation

Analyze the available context files and extract the following information:

## Information to Extract:

1. **Business Context**
   - What industry or domain is this for?
   - What is the customer's current business situation?
   - What challenges or pain points are they facing?
   - What opportunities exist for improvement or transformation?

2. **Stakeholder Information**
   - Who are the key stakeholders mentioned?
   - What are their roles and responsibilities?
   - What are their specific needs or concerns?
   - What customer segments or user groups are being served?

3. **Business Goals**
   - What business outcomes does the customer want to achieve?
   - Are there any specific metrics or KPIs mentioned?
   - What is the expected timeline or urgency?
   - What ROI or success expectations are outlined?

4. **Solution Context**
   - What type of solution is being considered?
   - Are there any technology preferences or constraints?
   - What is the scope of the solution?
   - What data sources and systems need to be integrated?

5. **Success Criteria**
   - How will success be measured?
   - What would constitute a successful outcome?
   - Are there any specific requirements or must-haves?
   - What are the key performance indicators mentioned?
   - What criteria would justify expanding or enhancing the solution?

## Output Format:

Create a document with the following sections:

**Product Vision Statement**
- A clear, concise vision statement (1-2 sentences) that captures the essence of what we're building and why

**Business Objectives**
- Primary objective (main business goal)
- Secondary objectives (supporting goals)
- Success metrics (how we'll measure achievement)

**Context Summary**
- Industry/domain context
- Key stakeholders and their needs
- Current challenges being addressed
- Solution scope and constraints

## Instructions:
1. Read through all available context files in the 000-context folder and 004-outputs folder carefully
2. Pay special attention to:
   - Initial requirements and business context
   - Use cases and their business value propositions
   - Data sources and system landscape
   - Implementation roadmap and success projections
3. Extract relevant information using the categories above
4. Synthesize the information into a coherent vision and objectives that align with the customer's needs
5. Ensure the vision is inspiring yet achievable for the specific industry/domain
6. Make objectives specific, measurable, and time-bound where possible
7. Reference specific use cases and business benefits from the context files
8. If information is missing, note what assumptions you're making

## Output Instructions:
Save your completed analysis as a markdown file named "01-product-vision-and-objectives.md" in the 004-Outputs folder.

Begin your analysis by stating: "Based on the context files in the 000-context folder and 004-outputs folder, I have extracted the following information for defining the product vision and objectives..."
